British Values Hub

The British Values Hub celebrates the principles that shape life in the City of Westminster— democracy, the rule of law, individual liberty, mutual respect, and tolerance of different faiths and beliefs.

This app helps residents, students, and visitors explore what these values mean in practice, discover how they influence our local community, and learn how each of us plays a part in upholding them.

From the heart of UK government to the diverse neighbourhoods that make Westminster unique, these values guide how we live, work, and grow together.

About

The British Values Hub was created to help everyone in the City of Westminster understand and engage with the principles that shape life in the UK. Rooted in the values of democracy, the rule of law, individual liberty, mutual respect, and tolerance of different faiths and beliefs, this app connects national ideals with the unique character of Westminster.

Our aim is to make these values meaningful, accessible, and relevant through local examples, interactive learning, and real stories from the people who live, work, and study here.
